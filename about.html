ABOUT ME
I'm Hoai Ngoc,
a UI UX Designer Intern
From a young age, I’ve always been curious and driven by my dreams. At 6, I wanted to own a grocery store, at 12, I dreamed of becoming a model, and by 18, I was determined to be a successful entrepreneur, earning a spot at a top economics university in the South Vietnam (UEH).
The turning point came at 20 during a university startup competition. I was inspired by a team’s idea of creating an app to help children develop their intellect through scientifically-backed games. This sparked my passion for UI/UX Design, leading me to explore the field deeply. I started from scratch, learning through online resources, mastering design principles, tools, and completing a 6-month course to refine my creative process.
Today, with a strong foundation in economics and problem-solving, I’m equipped with the skills to design seamless, user-centered experiences. I’m passionate about transforming bold ideas into intuitive and impactful designs, ready to bring these visions to life. Let’s create something amazing together!
